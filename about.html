The story

Two colleagues. One shared obsession.

How a friendship in Amsterdam became a bridge to the block printing workshops of Rajasthan.

Why Ghar

We started Ghar
because home
deserved better.

Not better as in more expensive — better as in more intentional. We wanted the things in our homes to have a story: made by someone whose name we knew, from fabric we could trace back to its source.

Ghar grew out of a friendship between two colleagues at AMI in Amsterdam, working directly with the Karghewale artisan community in Rajasthan.

"Every piece in our collection is made with love and intention. This is not a brand story. It is just what we believe."

Our makers

Meet Karghewale —
the heart of
our collection.

Our cotton canvas is sourced directly through Karghewale — an artisan community supporting the livelihoods, independence and heritage of traditional handloom weavers across Rajasthan. Master block printers then apply each print by hand, using carved wooden blocks and techniques passed down through generations.

The craft

From forest to your sofa —
how block printing works.

01 — Design

The pattern is born

A motif is sketched — a vine, a bloom, a geometric repeat. Every line is deliberate.

02 — Carve

Days of carving

The design is carved into teak by hand. One pattern can take up to 7 blocks — one per colour.

03 — Print

Pressed by hand

The inked block is pressed onto the cotton, colour by colour. Each impression is a human decision.

04 — Amsterdam

Cut, stitched, sent

Each piece is cut, stitched with contrasting piping, inspected — then sent from Jaipur to Amsterdam.

"The slight imperfections in the print are not mistakes. They are the mark of a human hand on every piece."

Why cotton?

Your skin knows
the difference.

We chose thick handwoven cotton canvas because it's the most honest material we could bring into your home. It breathes, softens with every wash, and returns to the earth at the end of its life.

🌬️ Breathable

Open weave lets air circulate — no synthetic heat-trapping.

🌱 Biodegradable

Returns to earth. Zero microplastics.

💧 Better with age

Softens with every wash; colours develop a patina.

🖐️ Holds print deeply

Natural fibres absorb dye all the way through.

Care with love

Made to last.
Loved always.

🌿

Hang outside to refresh first. It needs washing less often than you think.

🧊

If washing is needed: cold water, gentle cycle, alone.

🚫

No bleach. No tumble drying. (We mean it.)

☁️

Dry in the shade. Direct sunlight fades the colours.

🔥

Iron on the reverse side, slightly warm. Never directly on the print.
