A little care keeps hand block printed cotton beautiful for years.

The essentials

Five small habits.
Years of colour.

🌿

Hang outside to refresh first. It needs washing less often than you think.

🧊

If washing is needed: cold water, gentle cycle, alone.

🚫

No bleach. No tumble drying. (We mean it.)

☁️

Dry in the shade. Direct sunlight fades the colours.

🔥

Iron on the reverse side, slightly warm. Never directly on the print.
