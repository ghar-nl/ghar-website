In Hindi, घर is more than a building — it is the feeling of belonging. We make things for your home the way we'd want them in ours: slowly, with care, made to last.

The story

We started Ghar because home deserved better.

Two colleagues at AMI in Amsterdam, one shared obsession: bringing hand block printed textiles from Rajasthan into everyday slow living. We work directly with the Karghewale artisan community — fair wages, real craft, no shortcuts.
